An accessible way to access Corona statistics in India

This app allows to get data about corona infections in your State/District. If you want to directly get access to corona statistics, use your quick navigation keys to navigate to heading level 2 and find "Search for corona cases". Continue reading to know about contributers to this app.

Thanks to Pranav bhagwat, our colleague who first built this app. Pranav is part of our team working on Boardwalktech Platform. He built this app using Angular. Our Accessibility team came forward to make Accessibility enhancements and then we thought to host it on Tekvision domain.

If you have any suggestions to improve accessibility, post your suggestions on our Contact Us page. We will be more than happy to integrate those.
