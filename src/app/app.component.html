An accessible way to access Corona statistics in India

In this time of corona pandemic, it is important to "Stay Safe, Stay Home and Stay Healthy". It is our duty towards mankind to abide by restrictions for the safety of ourselves and our dear ones. In case you are visually challenged and struggling in any way during this pandemic, contact the nearest organization to seek help. Organizations like National Federation for the Blind and their state/district level bodies are extending a helping hand to those who are needy. Government is also ensuring that benefitiaries get the facilities and funds via the Jan Dhan Yojana. You can enquire about same in nearest Nationalized banks.

This app allows to get data about corona infections in your State/District. To directly get access to corona statistics, use your quick navigation keys to navigate to heading level 2 and find "Search for corona cases". Continue reading to know about contributers to this app.

Thanks to Pranav bhagwat, our colleague who first built this app. Pranav is part of our team working on Boardwalktech Platform. He built this app using Angular. Our Accessibility team came forward to make Accessibility enhancements and then we thought to host it on Tekvision domain.

We will try our level best to provide accessible data to users of Assistive Technology in India and keep updating this page accordingly. In case you are an organization willing to put forward your facilities and help on this app, reach out to us via our Contact Us page. If you have any suggestions to improve accessibility, post your suggestions on our Contact Us page. We will be more than happy to integrate those. Wherever you are, stay home and stay safe!
